What is Insureth?

One platform connecting parametric coverage, oracle verification, and capital provisioning.

Insureth is built to make parametric insurance understandable. Travelers can see what coverage they bought, when verification is expected, and why a payout did or did not happen. Investors support the pool in the background, but the customer experience stays simple and reassuring.

For travelers

Buy coverage with clear payout tiers, see verification progress after departure, and understand why a policy paid or did not pay.

For investors

Provide capital into the liquidity pool and view how premium inflows and payout events affect position performance.
